# HR Policy 2024

This document outlines HR policies applicable to all staff and the HR team.

## Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per calendar year.

## Performance Review Cycle

Reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December.

## Grievance Process

Contact HR directly or use the anonymous reporting portal.
